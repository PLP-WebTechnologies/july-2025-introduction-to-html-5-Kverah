My Vanilla Cake Recipe

This simple vanilla cake is soft,moist and perfect for any occassion

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup (200g) sugar
  2. 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  5. 1.5 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  6. 1.75 teaspoons baking powder
  7. 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  8. Pinch of salt

Description

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, cream together the sugar and butter until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until well combined.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool before removing from the pan. Serve plain or with your favorite frosting.
